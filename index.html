The Concurrent Calculi Formalisation Benchmark

Proposal

The publication of POPLMark spearheaded the era of publishing formal proofs alongside with new research. This fostered an era of high-quality and high-assurance proofs at top venues for programming language research. In this project we simultaneously acknowledge the impact of the original POPLMark challenge, and propose a new benchmark intended to stimulate the research and dissemination of techniques that address the particular challenges of process calculi and behavioural type systems. We propose three challenge problems concerning to process calculi and name passing, linearity in behavioural type systems, and coinductive reasoning for process algebras.
